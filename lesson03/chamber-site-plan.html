Chamber Site Plan

Site Name

Tooele Chamber

This site name was chosen for the town I have chosen of Tooele County, Utah. It is to respresent the Chamber in this city.

Site Purpose

The Tooele County Chamber of Commerce & Tourism page is for community members to find information about the chamber and how to contact the board.

This page also provides information for tourism in the area and provides visitors common info about Tooele County.

Scenarios

  • How do I find information on joining as a member of the chamber?
  • What events are held by this chamber and how can I be involved?
  • Where do I find contact information for the chamber?
